## World
[![upscale-00010.png](https://i.postimg.cc/m2RnHVPY/upscale-00010.png)](https://postimg.cc/9z813dt0)
Valik'thull is an ethereal plane that exists alongside and sometimes intertwined with other physical planes such as the one where Earth resides. It is a place of where magic is allowed to be more fluid. It's people have long since known that 'magic' as its known is one of the fundamental building blocks of reality. This ethereal plane of existence is comprised of seven major domains that each have their own elemental affinities.
Valik'thuul exists outside of the normal planes of existence, it is a place of raw magic that has not been distilled into matter. The Domain's themselves have been forged and molded by the inhabitants of the world.
The Seven Domains:
1. **An Ciúnas Mór** (Domain of Space)
2. **Sioraíocht** (Domain of Time)
3. **Anam** (Domain of Soul)
4. **Talamh** (Domain of Earth)
5. **Aer** (Domain of Air)
6. **Uisce** (Domain of Water)
7. [Aodh](/worlds/valikthuul/domain-of-fire) (Domain of Fire)

The Domains are relatively small bubbles of matter, they were created by previous Princes. These seven domains are each suited for the power of the each individual Prince. Outside of these domains is raw magic, the building blocks of everything. This area is untamed and wild, the only beings able to survive in it are powerful magic wielders. The Princes of Space, Time, and Soul have always been the dominant strengths. These three Domains form the 'core' tetrahedral of Valik'thuul's inhabitable area, with the other four (Fire, Water, Earth, Air) Domains arranged around them in a diamond pattern. Each Domain has a discrete border, travel between them is facilitated by teleportation arrays that were created and maintained by the generations of Space Princes.
Each of these domains are rules by a Prince (not all male), all seven eternally vying for the right to ascension into Godhood. In the history of Valik'thuul not a single Prince has managed to ascend.
The Seven Domains are not typically in open and active war, but there are constant power struggles and political machinations. In terms of raw power the top three have similar potential, and the bottom four are typically squabbling amongst themselves.
The domains and their 'royal' families do not have family names. They are distinguished by the power of their house and the magic in their bloodlines. Each prince is known simply as the Heir of their individual Domains until they come into their power and choose a name for themselves. A new heir has not been born in half a century.
